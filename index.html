Projects

Safe Entry

Safe Entry is a initiative by the Singapore Government to curb the spread of COVID-19 within Singapore. This app was created when people could use their internet browser to check into places. Hence, I implemented a webscraping element to receive and send data. Also, the app that the Singapore Government implemented did not track the user's location and most of the time the user forgot to check out of the places. This app can track user's location away from the check in location and remind the user to check out by sending a notification.
